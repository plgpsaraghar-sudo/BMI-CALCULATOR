About Us

We are a group of students working on a BMI project to spread awareness about health and fitness among school children aged 6–18. Our goal is to help boys and girls understand BMI and encourage healthy lifestyle habits from an early age..

Our Sacred Mission

Our sacred mission is to spread awareness about health and fitness among school students aged 6–18. We aim to educate boys and girls about BMI so they can grow healthier, stronger, and more confident for the future.
